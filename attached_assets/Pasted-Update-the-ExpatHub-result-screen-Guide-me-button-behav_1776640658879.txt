## Guide me button logic

Each blocker card has a "Guide me →" button with a guideMeLabel string.

Implement the following behaviour in two layers:

### Layer 1 — Paywall first
On Guide me button press:
- Trigger the existing RevenueCat paywall
- Pass the guideMeLabel as context to personalise the paywall headline
- The paywall headline should read: "Unlock: [guideMeLabel]"
  e.g. "Unlock: Help me build a savings runway plan"
- If the user subscribes or already has an active subscription, 
  proceed immediately to Layer 2
- If the user dismisses the paywall without subscribing, do nothing

### Layer 2 — In-app navigation after subscription confirmed
After RevenueCat confirms an active entitlement, navigate based on 
the guideMeLabel value using this mapping:

"Help me calculate my real moving budget" → app/tools/budget-calculator
"Show me cost of living for my top region" → app/content/cost-of-living
"Help me build a savings runway plan" → app/content/cost-of-living
"Show me income requirements for my target visa" → app/content/visa-guides
"Check if my income qualifies" → app/content/visa-guides
"Help me map an income transition plan" → app/content/visa-guides
"Match me to the right visa" → app/content/visa-guides
"Help me figure out which visa type I need" → app/content/visa-guides
"Start my visa research" → app/content/visa-guides
"What professional help do I need and what does it cost" → app/content/bureaucracy-guides
"Show me what the paperwork process actually looks like" → app/content/bureaucracy-guides
"Show me lower-bureaucracy destinations" → app/content/bureaucracy-guides
"Help us research the practical open questions" → app/content/destination-guides
"Help me think through what my household needs from a destination" → app/content/destination-guides
"Help me understand what's driving the hesitation" → app/content/destination-guides
"Show me what daily life actually looks like in my target region" → app/content/destination-guides
"Show me real expat accounts from my target region" → app/content/destination-guides
"Help me find destinations that fit my lifestyle requirements" → app/content/destination-guides
"Walk me through the exit strategy checklist" → app/tools/move-timeline
"Help me think through a realistic fallback plan" → app/tools/move-timeline
"Add this to my move timeline" → app/tools/move-timeline
"Help me build a backwards timeline from my target date" → app/tools/move-timeline
"Help me take my first concrete step" → app/tools/move-timeline
"Help me figure out what's actually in the way" → app/tools/move-timeline

If a screen does not yet exist, navigate to app/content/coming-soon 
and pass the guideMeLabel as a param so the screen can display:
"[guideMeLabel] — coming soon. You'll be notified when this is ready."

### Notes
- Check for active RevenueCat entitlement before triggering the paywall — 
  if the user is already subscribed, skip the paywall and go straight to 
  Layer 2 navigation
- Do not create any of the destination screens — only implement the 
  navigation logic and the coming-soon fallback
- Do not change any other result screen behaviour
