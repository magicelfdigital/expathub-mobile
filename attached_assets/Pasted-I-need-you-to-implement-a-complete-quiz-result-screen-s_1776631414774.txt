### Score calculation

For Q1–Q8:
- Yes = 2 pts
- Somewhat = 1 pt
- Not sure yet = 1 pt
- No = 0 pts

Weighted questions (multiply pts × weight before summing):
- Q1 Financial Cushion: 1.5×
- Q2 Income Stability: 1.5×
- Q3 Visa Pathway: 1.5×
- Q4–Q8: 1×

Max raw score = (2×1.5) + (2×1.5) + (2×1.5) + (2×1) + (2×1) + (2×1) + (2×1) + (2×1) = 19
Scale the score to display out of 16: displayScore = Math.round((rawScore / 19) * 16)

### Tier logic

- 0–5: Dreaming
- 6–11: Exploring  
- 12–16: Ready to Act
