Option 3 will drop fewer users. Here's why:

Asking for email + password **before** showing the result feels like a toll booth — users haven't seen value yet and will bail. Showing the result first creates the "I want to save this" motivation that makes account creation feel worthwhile rather than annoying.

**Recommended flow:**

> Quiz → Result (with Top Match) → "Create a free account to save your results" → Full registration (email + password) → Country list

The result screen is the hook. Account creation converts better when users are already emotionally invested in their match.
